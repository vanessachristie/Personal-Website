Ponose Cashier Application

Our team was tasked with creating a new cashier application for Ponose Coffee during our System Analyst & Design class. We conducted interviews and observations to identify the problems faced by the coffee shop. We discovered that Ponose Coffee had difficulties with their operational costs for their cashier application. They were still using manual bookkeeping for their transaction data, and the annual fee of their current cashier system was too expensive. Ponose Coffee also wanted additional features such as a membership system, stock and inventory management, and a way to view their sales data in graphical form. Our team developed a new cashier application that addressed Ponose Coffee's needs. We included an automatic sales data storage feature to replace the manual bookkeeping system. We also designed the application to have a more affordable annual fee compared to the previous system. We incorporated a membership feature, stock and inventory management system, and a graphical representation of monthly and yearly sales. The new cashier application we created helped Ponose Coffee streamline their operations and reduce their operational costs. They were able to store sales data more efficiently and access their monthly and yearly sales data in graphical form. The membership feature and stock and inventory management system also helped them manage their customers and inventory more effectively. Overall, the new cashier application improved Ponose Coffee's business operations and reduced their operational costs.
